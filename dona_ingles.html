To start making the dough for donuts you should place the flour in a large bowl and add the milk and yeast in the center. We will do what is called a flour volcano.              This done, melt the butter in the microwave and mix with the flour and the rest of the ingredients. Knead everything together until you get a smooth and elastic texture, that does not stick in your hands.              When the donut dough does not stay stuck in your hands, make a ball with it and leave it inside the container so that it rests for an hour and doubles its volume. It is advisable that you cover the dough with a clean cloth or plastic wrap.              Remember that this dough will serve to make homemade baked donuts and also fried donuts if you prefer.              After this time, the dough must have doubled its volume. Then, tap it a little on a flat surface to degas it. Then, stretch the dough with a rolling pin leaving it about 2 centimeters thick.              With the dough already stretched, cut the donuts with the desired diameter and see them reserving them on a tray lined with vegetal paper or sulfurized.              When you have the easy homemade donuts already cut you should let them rest for 30 more minutes to increase their size.

  • 1 kg of wheat flour
  • 260 grams of sugar
  • 40 grams of fresh yeast
  • 80 grams of butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 200 milliliters of milk
