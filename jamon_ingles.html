Pour all the wet ingredients into a bowl and mix well. Add flour and sugar to obtain a homogeneous and somewhat liquid consistency. Grease the entire surface of a pan and light over low heat. Once hot, pour enough in the center so that the mixture extends completely in a thin layer. Cook until small bubbles form; flip and cook on the other side. Do the same for the crepes you need. To avoid sticking, the pan should not be too hot.

  • 1 cup of wheat flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Salt
