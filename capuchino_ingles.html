This cappuccino coffee recipe can be prepared hot or cold, in both cases it will be delicious. The first of the ingredients to keep in mind is to prepare a good cup of coffee. If you have an Italian or espresso coffee machine, a trick to make a delicious coffee is to add half a spoonful of cinnamon to the mixture. It will bring a sweet taste.           Once we have the coffee ready, we add the sugar to taste and start preparing the presentation of this delicious dish. In a glass cup we put in coffee in the background. We add the hot milk if we give a touch will make us a little foam, something necessary to give it more eye-catching.           Finally, we topped this coffee with whipped cream and a bit of grated or powdered chocolate. These ingredients are those that sweeten a coffee that becomes the undisputed king of all the tables. Enjoy one of the best coffees in the world in the company of those you love most or in solitude of a special day.

  • A cup of milk
  • Grated or powdered chocolate
  • sugar
  • Half a tablespoon of cinnamon powder
  • Half a cup of cream
  • A cup of black coffee
