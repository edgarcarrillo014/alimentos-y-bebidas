Add the standard butter and sugar in the blender. Mix well at low speed for 1 minute, then rise at an average speed of 5 to 10 minutes until cream.   Add egg, vanilla, evaporated milk, coloring, water and beat for 2 min at low speed.   Then add Maribel Rice Flour, baking powder and mix at low speed for 1 minute until a manageable dough.   We form the figure of 40 - 45gr and put them on the trays.   Preheat the oven to 200 ° C for 15 minutes.   Bake 12 to 15 min.

  • 625 gr. Rice Flour
  • 210 gr of sugar
  • 125 ml of water
  • 20gr baking powder
  • 250gr of butter
  • 250 ml egg
  • vanilla to taste
  • Evaporated milk can
  • Pink coloring 5 ml
